WCI Scoring — How Points Are Calculated

Analyzer Metrics

Metric Meaning
Total WCI Average (Auto + Teleop + Endgame) WCI across all scouted matches for this team.
Σ VAR Standard deviation of the team's per-match WCI. Lower = more predictable. A team scoring 5-5-5 is a safer pick than 1-5-9.
Trend Recent-half average vs early-half average. Positive = improving across the event; negative = declining.
CONF Data confidence: HIGH ≥ 0.7  |  MED ≥ 0.4  |  LOW < 0.4.
Formula: 45% match count (8+ = full) + 30% scout diversity (3+ scouts = full) + 25% variance (low variance boosts it).
Tier Percentile rank within the current dataset:
S top 5%  •  A top 6–20%  •  B top 21–50%  •  C 51–80%  •  D bottom 20%
Role Auto-detected from scoring pattern:
🛡 Defender — plays defense ≥ 40% of matches with good effectiveness
⬆ Climber — endgame is ≥ 35% of total WCI and avg endgame ≥ 3 pts
⭐ All-Round — avg auto ≥ 3, teleop ≥ 4, endgame ≥ 2
🎯 Scorer — default (primarily a shooter/fuel scorer)
N Number of scouted matches for this team. Tiers and confidence improve with more data.
